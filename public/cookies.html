Cookie Policy

How we use small data to build a better news experience.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use our site.

How PrimeReport Uses Cookies

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function properly. These do not store personal data.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand visitor count and popular topics to improve our news coverage.

Advertising Cookies

Used by third-party ad networks to serve relevant ads. Refer to our Ad Choices for management.

Managing Your Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, though this may impact your experience on PrimeReport.
